1. Introduction

Welcome to Ak notes app. We are committed to respecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how our application functions and assures you that your personal data remains private.

This app is designed to allow users to create, edit, and view their personal notes efficiently. It functions without requiring any user registration, and we do not collect personal information.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Key Privacy Point: We do NOT collect, store, or share your personal data.

Our application is designed with privacy as a priority. We explicitly declare that we do not collect any of the following information:

  • Names, Emails, or Phone Numbers: We do not ask for your identity.
  • Account Details: There is no sign-up or login system.
  • Contact Lists: We do not access your address book.
  • Media & Files: We do not access your photos or files (other than the app's own data).
  • Location Data: We do not track your precise location, address, or GPS coordinates.
  • Device Identifiers: We do not collect IP addresses or unique device IDs for tracking purposes.

Non-Personal Statistics:
If the app ever generates internal statistics (such as a total count of notes created or generic usage metrics), these are strictly non-personal. This data cannot identify any individual user. Such data is visible only to the developer (admin) and is used exclusively for the technical improvement of the app's quality and performance.

3. Permissions & Internet Usage

Internet Connection:
The app uses your device's internet connection solely for essential functions, such as syncing your notes or loading necessary app resources. During this process, no personal data is transmitted to the developer, and no user activity is tracked.

App Permissions:
To protect your privacy, the app requires minimal permissions to function. We DO NOT request access to sensitive permissions such as:

  • Camera or Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Location Services (GPS)
  • External Storage (reading personal files/gallery)
  • SMS or Call Logs

4. Advertisements & Third-Party Services

We believe in a clean, distraction-free user experience.

  • 100% Ad-Free: There are no banner ads, interstitial ads, or rewarded video ads in this application.
  • No Third-Party SDKs: We do not integrate with third-party advertising networks, social media login SDKs, or external analytics services.
  • No Tracking: There is no profiling, cross-app tracking, or cross-site tracking of our users.

5. Children’s Privacy / Age Limitation

Ak notes app does not contain harmful, explicit, or inappropriate content and is safe for general audiences.

However, the app is intended for use by individuals aged 13 and above. Since we do not collect any personal data (including age or date of birth), we do not knowingly hold information about children. While the app poses no privacy risk to younger users, we recommend parental guidance for any mobile application usage.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app's functionality or legal requirements. Whenever a change is made, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
